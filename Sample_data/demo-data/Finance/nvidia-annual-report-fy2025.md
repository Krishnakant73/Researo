# NVIDIA Annual Report FY2025

**Author:** NVIDIA Corporation
**Category:** Finance

## Overview

NVIDIA Corporation Annual Report on Form 10-K for the fiscal year ended January 26, 2025. Data Center revenue reached $47.5B, up 217% year over year. Total revenue was $60.9B with a net margin of 55%.

## Business

Our data center platform combines CUDA-accelerated computing, high-speed networking (NVLink, InfiniBand), and a full-stack software ecosystem. Enterprise AI adoption is the primary demand driver. Gaming, professional visualization, and automotive remain meaningful but secondary segments.

## Risk Factors

Export controls to select regions materially reduce addressable market. Concentration risk exists in hyperscale customers, with three customers accounting for over 40% of Data Center revenue. Supply constraints in advanced packaging capacity limit near-term upside.

## Management Discussion and Analysis

Gross margin expanded to 74.8% driven by mix shift toward higher-value data center systems. Operating expenses grew slower than revenue, resulting in operating leverage. Free cash flow reached $27B, funding an expanded share repurchase program.
