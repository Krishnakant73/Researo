# Tesla Annual Report 2024

**Author:** Tesla, Inc.
**Category:** Finance

## Overview

Tesla, Inc. 2024 Annual Report. We produce and sell fully electric vehicles and energy generation and storage products. Total revenue was $97.7B, with automotive contributing the majority.

## Business

Our mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy. Full Self-Driving (Supervised) achieved a 3.2x reduction in accidents per mile versus the US fleet average. Deliveries reached 1.79 million vehicles for the year.

## Risk Factors

Our business is highly dependent on the sales of the Model 3 and Model Y vehicles, and competition in electric vehicles is intensifying globally. Price competition in China and Europe pressured automotive gross margins during the year.

## Financial Overview

Automotive revenue was $80.9B; energy generation and storage revenue grew 108% year over year. Free cash flow was $3.6B. Energy storage deployments reached a record 31.4 GWh, becoming a growing contributor to gross profit.
