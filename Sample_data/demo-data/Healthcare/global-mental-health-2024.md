# Global Mental Health Outlook 2024

**Author:** World Health Collaborative
**Category:** Healthcare

## Summary

Mental health conditions affect an estimated 1 in 8 people worldwide. This report reviews prevalence, treatment gaps, and the emerging role of digital interventions across 60 countries.

## Prevalence and Burden

Depression and anxiety disorders remain the leading contributors to years lived with disability. The treatment gap exceeds 70% in low- and middle-income countries, where fewer than two mental health workers serve every 100,000 people.

## Digital Interventions

Guided digital cognitive behavioral therapy showed effect sizes comparable to in-person care for mild to moderate depression in controlled trials. Adherence, not efficacy, was the primary limiting factor, with dropout rates near 40% for unguided apps.

## Workforce and Access

Scaling community health worker programs increased access more cost-effectively than specialist-only models. Integrating mental health screening into primary care raised detection rates by roughly a third in pilot programs.
